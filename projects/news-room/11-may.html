May 1, 2020

  • At the beginning of May, the FDA granted an EUA to remdesivir after preliminary data that found it to be effective in recovery for individuals with advanced COVID-19 and lung involvement.
  • The FDA also broadened authorization of saliva based tests, granting an EUA to Rutgers Clinical Genomics Laboratory. This test made COVID-19 testing more accessible to those that are at home due to illness, quarantine, or high risk of infection.

2020五月一日

  • 在5月初，美国食品和药物管理局在初步数据发现其对晚期新冠肺炎病和肺部受累的个体的恢复有效后，授予了EUA对伦德西韦的许可。
  • 美国食品和药物管理局还扩大了唾液检测的授权，授予罗格斯大学临床基因组实验室EUA奖。这项检测使得新冠肺炎检测对那些因疾病、隔离或高感染风险而在家的人来说更加容易。

In China, restaurants are suffering as workers are laid off and people are forced to stay at home. This experience is similar to those of restaurants in America.

在中国，由于工人被解雇，人们被迫呆在家里，餐馆受到了影响。这种体验和美国的餐厅差不多。
