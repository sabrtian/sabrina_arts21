June 18, 2020

  • The New England Journal of Medicine and The Lancet retract 2 studies on the use of hydroxychloroquine in COVID-19, showing the difficulties of publishing accurate COVID-19 research.
  • The WHO stops testing hydroxychloroquine as a COVID-19 treatment because data showed that it did not reduce mortality rates.
  • The NIH also stops clinical trials of hydroxychloroquine as a COVID-19 treatment.

2020六月十八日

  • 《新英格兰医学杂志》和《柳叶刀》撤回了2项关于羟氯喹在新冠肺炎使用的研究，显示了发表准确的新冠肺炎研究的困难。
  • 世界卫生组织停止测试羟氯喹作为新冠肺炎疗法，因为数据显示它不能降低死亡率。
  • 国家卫生研究院也停止了羟氯喹作为新冠肺炎疗法的临床试验。

In thirteen weeks, California respondents reported over 800 incidents related to discrimination and harassment. These average about 10 per day, with 81 incidents of physical assault, 64 incidents of potential civil rights violations, and 90 incidents of discrimination against elderly Asian Americans. Reports include:

  • "Someone threw a glass bottle at my friend while she was putting her baby in the car and yelled, "Go home, chink." (San Francisco)"
  • "Three Hispanic and two White individuals entered the store and cut in front of me. I spoke up and said, "Hey, I've been in line and I'm next." This opened up all of them to start calling me names and spitting on me. They said that they didn't care and that I needed to keep waiting. The cashier spoke up and said they had stop that and asked if I wanted to call the police. I declined and left the store immediately, not wanting any more confrontation. (Rosemead)"
  • "An attorney I work with made racially discriminatory comments about the opposing party, which was a Chinese business. He said the Chinese would be getting what they deserve and made other ethnically and racially discriminatory remarks against China, couched in the context of the opposing party being a Chinese business. (Los Angeles)"
  • "A YouTube commenter/creator wanted to call the disease "Chinese virus" and said he hated Chinese people. When I mentioned I am an American of ethnic Chinese descent, the commenter told me to go back to China. He said that I'm not an American and that America was made for Europeans and there should be ‘no Chinese mafioso’ in the USA. (San Francisco)"

在十三周内，加州受访者报告了800多起与歧视和骚扰相关的事件。这些事件平均每天约10起，其中81起是人身攻击事件，64起是潜在的侵犯公民权利事件，90起是对亚裔美国老人的歧视事件。报告包括:

  • "当我的朋友把她的孩子放进车里时，有人向她扔了一个玻璃瓶，并喊道:“回家吧，中国佬。”(旧金山)"
  • "三个西班牙人和两个白人走进商店，在我面前插队。我大声说:“嘿，我一直在排队，我是下一个。”这让他们所有人都开始骂我，向我吐口水。他们说他们不在乎，我需要继续等待。收银员大声说，他们已经停止了，并问我是否要报警。我谢绝了，立即离开了商店，不想再有任何对抗。(罗斯米德)"
  • "和我一起工作的一名律师对对方发表了带有种族歧视的评论，对方是一家中国公司。他说，中国人将得到他们应得的，并发表了其他针对中国的种族歧视言论，其背景是对方是中国企业。(洛杉矶)"
  • "一位YouTube评论者/创建者想把这种疾病称为“中国病毒”，并说他讨厌中国人。当我提到我是一个华裔美国人时，评论者告诉我回到中国。他说我不是美国人，美国是为欧洲人而生的，美国不应该有“中国黑手党”。(旧金山)"
