September 14, 2020

  • The government says it will stop screening international travelers that have been ongoing since January. This includes taking temperatures and being subject to a basic health screening about common COVID-19 symptoms before entering passport control and customs.
  • Pfizer, BioNTech expand phase 3 trial of their COVID-19 vaccine to 44,000 people to increase data on safety and efficacy, and promote a more diverse population.
  • The Trump Administration releases a vaccine distribution plan created by HHS and DOD that aims to make it free for all vaccines starting in January 2021. It does not decide on who would be the first to receive the vaccine.

2020九月十四日

  • 政府表示，将停止筛查自1月份以来一直在进行的国际旅行者。这包括在进入护照检查站和海关之前测量体温和接受关于常见新冠肺炎症状的基本健康检查。
  • 辉瑞公司和生物技术公司将其新冠肺炎疫苗的第三阶段试验扩大到44，000人，以增加安全性和有效性的数据，并促进更多样化的人群。
  • 特朗普政府发布了一项由HHS和国防部制定的疫苗分配计划，旨在从2021年1月开始对所有疫苗免费。它没有决定谁将是第一个接受疫苗的人。

"The Most Beautiful Retrograde" is a Chinese TV drama that was created based on the real events of COVID-19 pandemic that started at the end of 2019. It was created by a well known screenwriter and director, and it's main song was sung by a Chinese celebrity as well. After it was released, netizens claimed it discriminated against women and exaggerated real life facts. But at this point, the Chinese population has started to look back and reflect on the pandemic, a sign of already overcoming it.

《最美逆行者》是一部中国电视剧，它是根据2019年底开始的新冠肺炎大流行的真实事件创作的。它是由一位著名的编剧和导演创作的，它的主题曲也是由一位中国名人演唱的。发布后，网友称其歧视女性，夸大现实生活事实。但在这一点上，中国人民已经开始回顾和反思这场流行病，这是已经克服它的迹象。
