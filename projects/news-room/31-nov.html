November 4, 2020

  • The U.S. hits another new record of 100,000 new COVID-19 cases reported in a single day. This leads to a shortage of N95 masks at health care facilities, causing workers to continue to reuse masks.
  • A study analyzed flu vaccination rates and predicts that sufficient community immunity will face difficulties. They had previously recorded lower vaccination rates in Black and Hispanic adults than White adults, and in younger populations as compared to elderly adults.
  • President-elect Biden announces his COVID-19 transition team and Pfizer releases data showing its vaccine trail was 90% effective.

2020十一月四日

  • 美国在一天内又创下了10万例新COVID-19病例的新纪录。这导致医疗机构的N95口罩短缺，导致工人继续重复使用口罩。
  • 一项研究分析了流感疫苗接种率，并预测充足的社区免疫将面临困难。他们以前记录的黑人和西班牙裔成年人的疫苗接种率低于白人成年人，年轻人的疫苗接种率低于老年人。
  • 当选总统拜登宣布他的新冠肺炎过渡团队和辉瑞公司发布的数据显示，其疫苗试验是90%有效。

ABC News begins FACEism, a series of videos aimed to "expose our often-ignored history, erase stereotyping and move toward a better understanding of each other," and also feature Asian American reporters experience during this pandemic.

美国广播公司新闻开始了面部主义，这是一系列旨在“揭露我们经常被忽视的历史，消除陈规定型观念，走向更好地理解彼此”的视频，也是亚裔美国记者在这场流行病期间的经历。
