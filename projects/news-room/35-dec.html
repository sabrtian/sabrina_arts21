December 2020

  • The FDA advisory panel recommends the Pfizer, BioNTech vaccine, the first COVID-19 vaccine to be endorsed. They decide the benefits outweigh the risks for those age 16 and older.
  • A week later, the same FDA advisory panel also recommends Modern’s vaccine since the benefits outweigh the risks for those aged 18 years and older.
  • The UK announces that a new strain of the virus is present in the country. While it is more contagious, it has not proven to be more deadly.
  • The Trump administration buys 100 million more doses of Pfizer and BioNTech’s vaccine.
  • A new COVID-19 variant is found in Colorado in a 20 year old with no travel history.
  • By the end of the year, about 2.8 million people have been vaccinated.

2020十二月

  • 美国食品和药物管理局顾问小组推荐辉瑞公司的生物技术疫苗，这是第一种获得批准的新冠肺炎疫苗。他们认为对16岁及以上的人来说，利大于弊。
  • 一周后，同一个美国食品和药物管理局咨询小组也建议使用现代疫苗，因为对18岁及以上的人来说，益处大于风险。
  • 英国宣布该国出现了一种新的病毒。虽然传染性更强，但还没有证明它更致命。
  • 特朗普政府再购买1亿剂辉瑞和生物技术公司的疫苗。
  • 一种新的新冠肺炎变种在科罗拉多州被发现，年龄为20岁，没有旅行史。
  • 到年底，大约有280万人接种了疫苗。

With the different vaccine options and uncertainty surrounding it, the responses to whether or not we should all get vaccined receives a wide range of responses. Some medical experts try to utilize social media to push others to get the vaccine when available, while many are still skeptical of the side effects.
