  • At the beginning of April, Trump promoted the malaria drug hydroxychloroquine as possible treatments for COVID-19. With the common antibiotic azithromycin, the drug cocktail becomes an early solution to prevent hospitalization or death, but is soon warned by the American Heart Association, the American College of Cardiology, and the Heart Rhythm Society to warn that it is not suitable for everyone.

  • 4月初，特朗普推广疟疾药物羟氯喹作为新冠肺炎的可能治疗方法。与常见的抗生素阿奇霉素一样，药物鸡尾酒成为预防住院或死亡的早期解决方案，但很快被美国心脏协会、美国心脏病学会和心律协会警告说，它不适合每个人。

As U.S. cases spike to report twice the amount of cases as China, there is increasing speculation that China is concealing the extent of their COVID-19 outbreak. China isn't the only one who has been suspected of under-reporting. Other suspected countries include Iran, Russia, Indonesia, Saudi Arabia, Egypt, and especially North Korea, which has not reported a single case.

随着美国病例激增至中国病例的两倍，越来越多的人猜测中国正在隐瞒其新冠肺炎疫情的程度。中国并不是唯一一个被怀疑漏报的国家。其他疑似国家包括伊朗、俄罗斯、印度尼西亚、沙特阿拉伯、埃及，特别是朝鲜，该国尚未报告一例病例。
