June 22, 2020

  • A study in Science Translation Medicine suggests that possibly 80% of Americans who thought they had flu-like illnesses in March were actually infected COVID-19. This study also suggests that if ⅓ of these patients were tested for COVID-19, then there may have been up to 8.7 million infections reported.

2020六月十八日

  • 《科学翻译医学》杂志的一项研究表明，可能80%的美国人认为他们在三月份患有流感样疾病，实际上是感染了新冠肺炎。这项研究还表明，如果对这些患者中的⅓进行新冠肺炎检测，那么报告的感染人数可能高达870万。
