Early February 2020

  • Many countries start to impose air-travel restrictions against China, and Mainland China visitors are required to undergo health screenings.
  • The Trump administration declares a public health emergency 3 days after the WHO declared a Global Health emergency as there have been over 9800 cases and 200 deaths confirmed worldwide.

2020二月初

  • 许多国家开始对中国实施空中旅行限制，中国游客需要接受健康检查。
  • 特朗普政府在世卫组织宣布全球卫生紧急情况三天后宣布公共卫生紧急情况，因为全球已有9800多起病例和200例死亡得到确认。

As China’s COVID-19 deaths exceed those of the SARS crisis from 17 years ago, Chinese celebrities work together to compose music, write lyrics, and sing songs of encouragement, cheering on doctors and those in quarantine.

随着中国COVID-19的死亡人数超过17年前非典危机的死亡人数，中国名人一起创作音乐，写歌词，唱鼓励的歌曲，为医生和隔离人员欢呼。
