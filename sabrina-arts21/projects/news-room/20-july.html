July 22, 2020

  • HHS and Department of Defense partner with Pfizer and BioNTech for a December delivery of 100 million doses of their COVID-19 vaccine candidate.
  • Research from the New England Journal of Medicine shows that antibody levels drop dramatically after the first 3 months of infection. Researchers note that antibody resistance would be depleted within a year, but experts think that the possibility of being infected again is unlikely.
  • The Moderna Vaccine begins its Phase 3 Trial, and receives $472 million from the Trump Administration.
  • Senate introduces HEALS Act, a package of bills that will provide provisions for another stimulus check, more money for small businesses, and liability protections for companies that are bringing employees into the workplace.

2020七月二十二日

  • HHS和国防部与辉瑞和生物技术公司合作，于12月交付了1亿剂他们的COVID-19候选疫苗。
  • 《新英格兰医学杂志》的研究表明，感染后的前3个月，抗体水平急剧下降。研究人员指出，抗体耐药性将在一年内耗尽，但专家认为再次感染的可能性不大。
  • 现代疫苗开始第三阶段试验，并从特朗普政府获得4.72亿美元。
  • 参议院引入了医疗保险法案，这是一系列法案，将为另一项刺激计划提供资金，为小企业提供更多资金，并为将员工带入工作场所的公司提供责任保护。

Unfavorable views of China from Americans also reaches a high.

美国人对中国的不利看法也达到了很高的水平。
