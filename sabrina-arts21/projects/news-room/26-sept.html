The Stop AAPI Hate Youth Campaign publishes a study in which they interviewed 990 AAPI young adults across the U.S. and shared their experiences related to racist reactions of the COVID-19 pandemic. Through this, they were able to find that about 77% of these youths expressed anger over the current anti-Asian hate, they were most likely to be concerned about their family, and they faced harassment focused on blaming China and Chinese people and mocking Chinese dietary habits.
