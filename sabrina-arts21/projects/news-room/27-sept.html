September 23, 2020

  • A study at houston Methodist Hospital discovers a more contagious strain of COVID-19. Continued research finds that almost all strains have mutated to bind and infect more cells.
  • The Midwest sees a drastic increase in COVID-19 cases due to the annual Sturgis motorcycle rally, school and university reopenings, and Labor Day weekend celebrations.
  • Global COVID-19 deaths surpass 1 million, which is more than the deaths caused by HIV, dysentery, malaria, influenza, cholera, and measles combined in 2020.
  • In order to assist K-12 schools in reopening, the HHS announces a plan to distribute 100 million rapid tests to states by the end of 2020.

2020九月二十三日

  • 休斯顿卫理公会医院的一项研究发现了一种更具传染性的新冠肺炎病毒。持续的研究发现，几乎所有的菌株都发生了突变，结合并感染了更多的细胞。
  • 由于一年一度的斯特吉斯摩托车拉力赛、学校和大学重新开放以及劳动节周末庆祝活动，中西部地区的新冠肺炎病例急剧增加。
  • 全球新冠肺炎死亡人数超过100万，超过2020年由艾滋病毒、痢疾、疟疾、流感、霍乱和麻疹导致的死亡人数总和。
  • 为了帮助K-12学校重新开学，HHS宣布了一项计划，计划在2020年底前向各州分发1亿份快速测试。

China releases a new TV show called "With You", consisting of 20 total episodes broken down into 10 units. It is adapted from the real story of China during the few months that they fought against the COVID-19 pandemic. Each unit consists of famous Chinese actors and actresses who volunteered in producing the show, as a celebration of their efforts and gratitude towards their healthcare workers.

中国将推出一档名为《在一起》的新电视节目，共20集，分为10个单元。它改编自中国抗击新冠肺炎大流行的几个月中的真实故事。每个单位都由志愿制作这部剧的中国著名男女演员组成，以此来庆祝他们的努力和对医护人员的感谢。
