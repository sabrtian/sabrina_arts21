November 16, 2020

  • Moderna announces that its vaccine has a 94.5% effectiveness. Both the Moderna and Pfizer vaccine use mRNA, which is unprecedented in approved vaccines. The FDA says it will move as quickly as possible to clear Pfizer and Moderna’s vaccine for emergency uses.
  • Fauci discusses the long term effects on cardiovascular systems with the American Heart Association, and emphasizes the need to follow up with patients to fully understand this.

2020十一月十六日

  • 莫德纳宣布其疫苗有94.5%的有效性。莫德纳和辉瑞的疫苗都使用基因，这在批准的疫苗中是前所未有的。美国食品和药物管理局表示，将尽快批准辉瑞和莫德纳的疫苗用于紧急用途。
  • 福奇与美国心脏协会讨论了对心血管系统的长期影响，并强调需要对患者进行随访以充分了解这一点。

While Americans are trying to move along with the vaccine, many Chinese people are sharing their experience in the pandemic, their lost family members, participating in vaccine trials, and how it's affected them now after they don't have to quarantine through writing and videos on social media.

当美国人试图推进疫苗时，许多中国人正在分享他们在大流行中的经历，他们失去的家人，参与疫苗试验，以及在他们不必通过社交媒体上的文字和视频进行隔离后，它现在如何影响他们。
