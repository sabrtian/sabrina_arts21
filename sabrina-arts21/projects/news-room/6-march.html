Many universities have either had or are about to start their Spring Break, causing them to make the decision to send all students home for the remainder of the semester, with the exception of those who face travel restrictions. However, this places extra stress and pressure on international students, Chinese ones in particular, who not only had a home in the U.S., were unable to receive on campus housing, were forced to move off campus with a few days notice, and could not afford hotels to quarantine in in China.
